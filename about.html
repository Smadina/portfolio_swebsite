Hi, glad you're here! My name is Madina Starks. I'm a multi-disciplinary designer who believes that good design is emotional design.


As a graphic designer wiith over five years of experience, I was involved in print campaigns creating materials that evoke users’ emotions through color, imagery, and typography. Over time, I felt that I want to explore different ways to expand my graphic design skills and transfer them into a new role. My curiosity led me to a user interface (UI) design, where I can evoke user’s emotions not only through the visual design but also through interactions and accessibility. After doing some research, I immersed myself in the CareerFoundry’s UI Design Program to learn how to design with the user in mind and create meaningful and intuitive interactions.


My experience with UI design taught me that while the appearance of the interface is the first thing that grabs user’s attention, designing the user experience (UX) is as much important as designing UI. I was able to not only apply my graphic design skills but, importantly, focus on creating the best possible user experience that includes functional design, interaction design, and accessibility of design.


If you're interested in my work, feel free to download my resume or contact me at madinastarks@gmail.com.
